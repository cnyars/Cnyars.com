Received a Letter From Us?

If you received a letter from Central New York Asset Recovery Solutions (CNYARS), you were contacted because publicly available records maintained by the New York State Office of Unclaimed Funds indicate that you may be associated with unclaimed property.

What Is Unclaimed Property?

  • Bank Accounts
  • Uncashed Checks
  • Utility Deposits
  • Insurance Proceeds
  • Stocks and Dividends
  • Mutual Funds
  • Other Financial Assets

How Do I Know My Letter Is Real?

The information included in your letter was obtained from publicly available records maintained by the New York State Office of Unclaimed Funds.

You may independently verify this information by searching the New York State Office of Unclaimed Funds database.

Search the New York State Unclaimed Funds Database

Do I Have To Use Your Service?

No.

You may pursue your claim directly through New York State at no cost. Our services are entirely optional.

What Does CNYARS Do?

  • Review claim requirements
  • Help gather supporting documentation
  • Assist with claim paperwork
  • Guide clients through the process

What Does It Cost?

Our fee is 15% of recovered funds, which is the maximum fee permitted under New York law.

If no funds are recovered, no fee is owed.
