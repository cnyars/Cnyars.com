If you received a letter from Central New York Asset Recovery Solutions (CNYARS), you were contacted because publicly available records maintained by the New York State Office of Unclaimed Funds indicate that you may be associated with unclaimed property.

The information included in your letter was obtained from public records maintained by New York State.

You do not have to use our service. You may pursue your claim directly through New York State at no cost.

How Can I Verify This?

You may independently search the New York State Office of Unclaimed Funds database using your name.

Search the Official NYS Database

What Does CNYARS Charge?

Our fee is 15% of recovered funds, which is the maximum fee permitted under New York law for abandoned property location services.

If no funds are recovered, no fee is owed.
