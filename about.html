Our Mission

Central New York Asset Recovery Solutions (CNYARS) was created to help individuals recover money and property that already belongs to them.

Every year, millions of dollars remain unclaimed because people move, change addresses, close accounts, or simply never realize funds are being held in their names.

We believe that money belongs in the hands of its rightful owners, not sitting forgotten in government databases.
